SOUTH TANGERANG

South Tangerang is a city located in the province of Banten, Indonesia. Being raised in South Tangerang means growing up in a suburban environment with access to modern amenities and facilities. The city is relatively new and has experienced rapid growth in the past few decades, leading to the development of numerous housing complexes, commercial areas, and public spaces.

As a resident of South Tangerang, you would have access to various schools, hospitals, shopping malls, and recreational facilities, such as parks and sports centers. The city is also well-connected to the capital city of Jakarta, which is only a short drive away.
